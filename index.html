Tian Mu, an exotic place in Taipei.

Tianmu is a neighbourhood located in the north of Taipei, the capital city of Taiwan. In the 19s, it is an enclave for Taiwan's US expatriate community. Thus, a great proportion of the US Army and their families stationed here, which made it a residential area with people from various cultural backgrounds. Also, Tainmu borders Yangming Mountain, a National Park in Taiwan. Surrounded by natural environments, Tainmu has become an ideal home for senior residents.
