Da-wei Kitchen

大為廚房 / Taiwanese Cuisine

Da-Wei Kitchen is a traditional restaurant which serves various Taiwanese dishes such as braised pork, steam fish, cabbage soup, and oil chicken. Without additional flavours, you can taste the original flavour of every food. The seafood dishes are highly recommended, which are fresh and well-seasoning.

Info

  • less oil & seasoning
