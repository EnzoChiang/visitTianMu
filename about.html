About website

This is a travel website for those planning for their trip to Tianmu, we are focusing on introducing some natural attractions and Taiwanese delicacies for people who want to experience the cultural diversity and the nature of Tainmu. The goal of this website is to promote my hometown, Tianmu and also provide some local advice and information for the foreign visitor. We hope all the resources we provided are useful and please enjoy your trip in Taiwan!
