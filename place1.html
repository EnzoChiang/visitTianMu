YangMing Mountain

陽明山 / National Park

YangMing Mountain is one of Taiwan's National Parks, which is also a volcano across Taipei and New Taipei city. It is a relaxing place for people in the city. In addition, it is home for many Taiwanese special species which deserves your visit.
